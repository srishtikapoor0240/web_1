Welcome to my website—yes, I actually made this instead of taking a nap, which already says a lot about my life choices. Think of this place as a curated museum of my existence, except without the overpriced tickets and painfully slow audio guides. Here, you’ll find my projects, ideas, and the occasional spark of genius that usually appears at 3 AM when I’m clearly supposed to be doing something responsible. I built this site partly to showcase my work, partly to convince myself that I am, in fact, a productive human being, and mostly because procrastinating feels less illegal when you make it look aesthetic.

So go ahead, explore, judge, admire, question your own life decisions—whatever feels right. I hope this little corner of the internet makes you smile, laugh, or at least say “wow, same.” Thanks for stopping by… and yes, I fully accept applause, compliments, and snacks.
